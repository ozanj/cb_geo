Figure Notes:
- Each point shows the relative percent change in the racial/ethnic composition of the remaining visible prospect pool after excluding the named Geomarket. Relative percent change is calculated as 100 × (remaining-pool share − full-pool share) / full-pool share. Positive values mean the remaining pool has higher relative representation than the full metro pool; negative values mean the remaining pool has lower relative representation. Facet titles report the full-pool count and baseline share for each racial/ethnic group. Excludes students with missing values for race/ethnicity.
