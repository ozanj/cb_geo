Figure Notes:
- Each point shows the percentage-point change in the race-by-first-generation composition of the remaining visible prospect pool after excluding the named Geomarket. Positive values mean the remaining pool has a higher share than the full metro pool; negative values mean the remaining pool has a lower share. Rows show racial/ethnic groups; columns distinguish first-generation and non-first-generation prospects. Within each racial/ethnic row, Geomarkets are sorted by the first-generation subgroup's percentage-point change. Panel labels report the full-pool count and baseline share for each race-by-first-generation subgroup. Excludes students with missing values for race/ethnicity or first-generation status.
