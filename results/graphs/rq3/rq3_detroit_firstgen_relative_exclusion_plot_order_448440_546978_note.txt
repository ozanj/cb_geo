Figure Notes:
- Each point shows the relative percent change in first-generation status composition of the remaining visible prospect pool after excluding the named Geomarket. Relative percent change is calculated as 100 × (remaining-pool share − full-pool share) / full-pool share. Positive values mean the remaining pool has higher relative representation than the full metro pool; negative values mean the remaining pool has lower relative representation. Facet titles report the full-pool count and baseline share for each first-generation status group. Excludes students with missing values for first-generation status.
